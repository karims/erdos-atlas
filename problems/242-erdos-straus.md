# Erdős Problem #242

## Title
Erdős–Straus conjecture

## Status
Open

## Source
https://www.erdosproblems.com/latex/242

## Problem statement

For every $n>2$ there exist distinct integers $1\leq x<y<z$ such that
\[
\frac{4}{n} = \frac{1}{x}+\frac{1}{y}+\frac{1}{z}.
\]

## Notes

This is the Erdős–Straus conjecture.
