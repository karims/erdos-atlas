# Erdős Problem #234

## Title
Normalized prime gaps

## Status
Open

## Source
https://www.erdosproblems.com/234

## Problem statement

For every $c\geq 0$ the density $f(c)$ of integers for which
\[
\frac{p_{n+1}-p_n}{\log n}< c
\]
exists and is a continuous function of $c$.

## Visual idea

This poster should explain how prime gaps are normalized, what the function $f(c)$ measures, and why the question is about the existence and continuity of a limiting distribution.

The Distribution of Normalized Prime Gaps
