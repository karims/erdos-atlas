Create a square mathematical art poster for Erdős Problem #242, the Erdős–Straus conjecture.

Use an elegant minimalist style with an ivory or off-white background, black mathematical typography, and subtle deep red accents.

Main visual idea:
A large central fraction “4/n” transforms or splits into three distinct unit fractions:
“1/x”, “1/y”, and “1/z”.

The composition should suggest decomposition, number-theoretic structure, and mystery.
The image should feel like a blend of clean modern mathematical poster design and subtle old-manuscript elegance.

Title:
“Three Unit Shadows”

Subtitle:
“Erdős Problem #242”

Small label:
“Erdős–Straus conjecture”

Problem statement:
“For every n > 2 there exist distinct integers 1 ≤ x < y < z such that
4/n = 1/x + 1/y + 1/z.”
